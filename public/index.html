About Test Realty

A Boutique Firm
Built on Trust

Test Realty is a boutique luxury real estate agency specialising in off-plan property investment across Dubai's most prestigious addresses. We combine deep market knowledge with personal service to guide both local and international investors.

RERA Licensed

Fully regulated and compliant with Dubai Real Estate Regulatory Authority standards, ensuring your investment is always protected.

Off-Plan Expertise

Early-access relationships with Dubai's leading developers give our clients first opportunity on launch-day pricing and premium units.

Global Clientele

Our Services

Comprehensive off-plan investment advisory for buyers and investors seeking the finest properties in Dubai.

01

Off-Plan Property Sales

Exclusive access to launch allocations across Dubai's top developments — from Creek Harbour to Palm Jebel Ali. Subject to DLD approval.

Golden Visa Guidance
